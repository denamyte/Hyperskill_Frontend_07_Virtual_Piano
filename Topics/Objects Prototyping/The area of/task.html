The area of...

Create two objects named rectangle and triangle that are prototyped from the shape object. These objects should print the area of the corresponding shapes.

Hint:

  • To calculate the area of a rectangle, multiply the length of the rectangle by its width.
  • To calculate the area of a triangle, multiply the length of the base by the height of the triangle and divide the result by 2.

24
15

Sample Output:
20
